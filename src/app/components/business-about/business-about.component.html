What the heck is a Coupost?

A coupost is a coupon in return for a post...pretty clever name right?
Our patent-pending process allows users to post about businesses on their accounts in return for a special deal or coupon to the business's store/website

How does it work?

1. Business creates a coupost

You create a coupost by offering a discount to a specified amount of users in return for posting a picture that you supply.

Ex: 40% OFF Online Order

2. User posts about business

A user must have a minimum of 200 followers. Users will select a business's coupost, choose an image to post on their Instagram Feed or Instagram Story and tag the business's Instagram account.

3. User Redeems

After our team has verified that the user has posted & complied with all the rules, we activate their coupost. Once activated, they're able to redeem the coupost through their profile on couposts.com.

The user can redeem using a promo code that is supplied by the business at the time the coupost was created or if it's an in-store redemption it can be a redeem button.

How much does it cost to run a Coupost?

Currently, you can run 2 types of coupost campaings. You can do a either a Pay Per Post or a Pay Per Engagement.
Pay Per Post
The user might have 200 followers, or they might have 1,500+. But you pay the same price per person. If 12 people post, you pay for the 12 posts. If 0 post then you don't owe anything.
You also have the option to "Pay as you go" or "Pay & Save". If you pay upfront then you save on the cost per post.
Pay Per Engagement
You sick of influencers with a million followers but only get 100 likes? With Pay per engagement, you only pay for the engagement (likes, views, comments) on the user's posts.
You also have the option to "Pay as you go" or "Pay & Save". If you pay upfront then you save on the cost per engagement.
